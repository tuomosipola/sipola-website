Belthandros and Chrysantza — The Greek text according to Adolf Ellissen

Belthandros and Chrysantza (Τα κατὰ Βέλθανδρον καὶ Χρυσάντζαν) is a Byzantine romance from the Middle Ages. This PDF book contains the Greek text from Adolf Ellissen's edition (Leipzig 1862). 45 pages. A4 210 × 297 mm. ISBN 978-952-68251-0-6 (PDF). NOTE: I am working on a corrected and revised version (books 1 and 2 done, 3rd in progress, i.e., 43% done on August 1 2015).
