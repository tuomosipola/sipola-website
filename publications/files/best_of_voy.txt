Best of Star Trek: Voyager
(1995 - 2001)

41 best episodes + 95 for added fun

Best episodes based on:
Jammer's reviews http://www.jammersreviews.com/st-voy/
IMDB user ranking http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0112178/eprate
Den of Geek episode roadmap http://www.denofgeek.com/tv/star-trek-voyager/38075/star-trek-voyager-an-episode-roadmap
Tuomo Sipola's personal preference

Episodes in parentheses () are additions that help with the different story arcs.
This is not such a huge problem with Voyager except for Doctor and Seven.
The tags show the different arcs and episode moods, and may contain spoilers.
The best characters are, again, Doctor and Seven, so follow these tags.
The series starts to get better at around S03E08 Future's End.

It is difficult to exclude episodes because Voyager is so mediocre throughout.
This was much easier with TNG. I had to add a three-star review column.
*** = watch it!
**  = nice addition
*   = just for continuity
