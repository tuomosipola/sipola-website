Best of Star Trek: The Next Generation
(1987 - 1994)

31 best episodes + 45 for added fun + 1 good movie + 1 for added fun

Best episodes based on:
Jammer's reviews http://www.jammersreviews.com/st-tng/
Max Temkin's recommendations https://medium.com/maxistentialism-blog/star-trek-the-next-generation-in-40-hours-c4a6762cbd3
IMDB user ranking http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0092455/epvote?ref_=ttep_ql_5
Tuomo Sipola's personal preference (so many fun episodes still left out)

Episodes in parentheses () are additions that help with the different story arcs.
Especially S02E20 The Emissary and S04E07 Reunion are relevant to Redemption.
The tags show the different arcs and episode moods, and may contain spoilers.
